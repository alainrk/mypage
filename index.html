Hello, I'm Alain Di Chiappari, a Software Engineer and Computer Scientist specialized in the design and implementation of Distributed Systems and Microservices Applications. I've been fascinated by computers since I was 6 years old, when I started coding using a basic version of Logo back in elementary school.

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work on a variety of interesting projects, ranging from web development to low-level systems and networks. I've gained experience in different industries and worked in companies of various sizes, including startups and large companies like Twilio and Hotjar.

Currently, I work as a Senior Software Engineer at Unobravo, where I'm involved in projects related to DevX, event-based microservices, infrastructure, observability, and more within the Platform team. I have a deep passion for technology and enjoy pushing the boundaries to create innovative, reliable, and scalable solutions.
